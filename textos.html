Lucas: Hi, I'm Lucas. What's your name?

Emma: Hi Lucas, I'm Emma. It's nice to meet you.

Lucas: Nice to meet you too, Emma. What grade are you in?

Emma: I'm in the seventh grade. How about you?

Lucas: I'm in the eighth grade. Do you like it here at school?

Emma: Yeah, it's pretty cool. I like my science class the most. What's your favorite subject?

Lucas: I really like math. I enjoy solving problems and figuring things out.

Emma: That's awesome! I find math a bit challenging sometimes.

Lucas: It can be tricky, but it's fun once you get the hang of it. Do you have any hobbies?

Lucas: I'm really into video games and basketball.

Emma: Cool, I play basketball too! Maybe we could play together sometime.

Lucas: That would be great! It was nice talking to you, Emma.

Emma: Likewise, Lucas. See you around!
